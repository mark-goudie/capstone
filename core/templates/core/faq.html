Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PrimaryED CMS.

Q: What types of educational resources are available in PrimaryED CMS?

A: PrimaryED CMS offers a diverse range of educational resources including interactive quizzes, detailed lesson plans, and engaging exercises tailored for primary education.

Q: Is PrimaryED CMS suitable for all primary education levels?

A: Yes, our platform is designed to cater to various levels of primary education, offering materials suitable for different age groups and learning stages.

Q: How can I access the quizzes and exercises?

A: Quizzes and exercises can be accessed by registering on our platform. Once registered, you can browse through our extensive library and select resources as per your needs.

Q: Can I contribute my own resources to PrimaryED CMS?

A: Absolutely! We encourage educators to contribute their own resources. Please contact us for more information on how to share your materials.

Q: Is there a subscription fee for using PrimaryED CMS?

A: PrimaryED CMS offers both free and premium content. While a significant portion of our resources are available free of charge, some specialised content requires a subscription.
